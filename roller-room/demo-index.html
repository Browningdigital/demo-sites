Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need a reservation for open skate? +
No reservation required for open sessions. We recommend calling 972-727-0027 to check availability on weekends and during special events.
Can I bring my own skates? +
Yes. Roller skates or inline skates are both welcome as long as they're in good condition. BYO skate admission is $10.
What ages are allowed? +
All ages are welcome. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Kids 12 and older can skate without supervision.
Do you offer skating lessons? +
Yes — Camp Skate runs Saturday mornings from 8 to 10 AM. $10 per class with skate rental included. Check the calendar for upcoming sessions.
What's the cancellation policy for parties? +
Cancellations must be requested at least 7 days before the event to reschedule. All deposits are non-refundable.
What items are not allowed? +
Alcohol, ice cream, glass containers, confetti, and glitter are not permitted in the venue.
